Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to point people to Jesus Christ. It will be an informative website that reinforces the doctrine taught by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Audience

The audience for the website will be anyone who is interested in learning more about the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
